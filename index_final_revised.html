About Me

I’m a student at HCC’s Auto Body and Collision Repair program with real hands-on experience in restoring vehicles to factory and custom condition.

In my work, I’ve rebuilt damaged body lines using filler and block sanding, performed dent repair on various panels, and prepped surfaces for refinishing. I’ve also created custom flame airbrush paint designs, hydro dipped emblems, removed and installed window regulators, and applied complete refinishes in a spray booth environment. Every step, from teardown to final polish, is done with attention to detail and a commitment to clean craftsmanship.
